ABOUT

I'm a automation tester (fancy name to say that I develop auto scripts to execute testing on websites and applications).
My first experience with QC job was 2007, as a manual tester for an e-Commerce company (website: kiemviec.com, now is known as careerbuilder.vn). At that time, QC job (aka Testing Service) is a very new in IT career. 2 years after that, I start my first automation task in a healthcare project with QTP tool (Quick Test Pro, now known as UFT - Unified Functional Testing).
The time has gone, the experience has come, the youth has fled. I'm now 35 with over 12 years experience in testing, including upto 8 years in automation testing. My most favorite is develop automation with Selenium in Java.
Analysis and Solving issue is one of strong in my skillset.
What do you want to automate, Why you are going to automate, and What the results are? The answers of those are my mindset before starting automate for a project.
Learning is Never Enough, especially with code!
